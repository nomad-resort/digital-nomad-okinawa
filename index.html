Why is Okinawa so special?

Ranked #1 in Happiness
by Prefecture in Japan

Okinawa offers a pleasant climate year-round, with stunning natural landscapes that include oceans, mountains, and rivers.
As Japan’s most famous resort destination, Okinawa is also home to some of the longest-living people in the world.
